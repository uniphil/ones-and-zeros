About

Ones and Zeros is an independent art-centred tech community, committed to serving people who don't normally see themselves represented in tech spaces. Our workshops and classes introduce electronics and coding with a critical lens and a focus on creativity.

We don't have a permanent physical space (for now!) – we partner with other organizations to reach creative folks all over the country. Get in touch to see how we can work together.

Community sponsors
